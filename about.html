Our Story

Built for Entrepreneurs Who Move Fast

Waparcel was founded to solve a real problem: Malaysian businesses importing from China faced fragmented logistics — different agents for sourcing, shipping, customs, and warehousing. That meant delays, hidden costs, and quality control nightmares.

We built the complete infrastructure — our own warehouse in Selangor, relationships with factories across China, and systems that integrate directly with your sales platforms. Now you get one partner, one dashboard, one relationship.

Whether you're dropshipping on Shopee, running wholesale operations, or scaling a brand across Southeast Asia — Waparcel gives you the operational backbone to grow without limits.
